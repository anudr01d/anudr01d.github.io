Echoes of silence

27 September 2016

"Write about love!", said the voice that was blind to pain.

"Write about your insignificance", cried the voice that was in search of it's identity.

"Write about life", murmured the voice that chose to see the depths rather than ripples on the surface.

But the loudest of them all was the voice that heard the echoes of silence.
