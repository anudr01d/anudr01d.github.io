The Ethereal

13 September 2016 on Getting Started

Calm shores where time met tide
Moments of clarity welcomed waves of delirium.
Where the light doesn't fall, would you dare try seeing?
For it was the darkness that shone on a soul and cast a shadow of my being.

Anudeep
