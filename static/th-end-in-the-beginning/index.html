The end in the beginning

15 September 2016

Losing touch of reality like a sane soul skinned alive..
The battle survived, feathers plucked, but not the will to fly..
They can only make believers live in a land of make-believe,
Seeing is not believing in a world of dreams...., The society ill, as it left the hopes diseased..
What good is a look through the window of truth, when living a lie?!
What's peace to a land with none of its children as allies..
Whats tomorrow for a today that lived to die..

..and yet you seek to try??

Anudeep
